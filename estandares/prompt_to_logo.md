# Prompt de Replicación Maestra - Logo MetodologIA

Este documento define el estándar visual exacto para el logo de MetodologIA, asegurando consistencia entre versiones SVG, PNG y generación por IA.

## Prompt Maestro de Generación (Fidelidad Total)

> "A hyper-minimalist 2D logo for an AI consultancy 'MetodologIA'. The logo is set inside a rounded square (squircle) with a deep navy blue linear gradient (#0A122A to #1E293B). In the center of the squircle, there is a white geometric icon consisting of three vertical columns representing data and stability:
> 
> 1. Left column: A solid continuous vertical bar.
> 2. Middle column: Split into a long top section and a small square bottom section, separated by a thin gap.
> 3. Right column: Split into a medium top section and a medium bottom section, with the gap positioned higher than in the middle column.
> 
> Directly above the middle column, centered, there is a single, perfectly round, solid gold circle (#FFD700) floating with a subtle glow. The aesthetic is ultra-clean, pixel-perfect, and professional. Flat corporate design with a tech-evolution edge."

## Especificaciones Geométricas (Base 36x36)

Para replicar el logo con precisión técnica (SVG):

- **Contenedor:** Rectángulo de 36x36 con bordes redondeados (`rx="10"`).
- **Fondo:** Degradado `#0A122A` → `#1E293B`.
- **Iconografía (Pillars):**
    - **Pilar 1 (x:10):** Alto total (y:12 a 24).
    - **Pilar 2 (x:16):** Cuerpo superior (y:12 a 20) + Base mínima (y:22 a 24).
    - **Pilar 3 (x:22):** Cuerpo superior (y:12 a 18) + Base media (y:20 a 24).
- **El Punto de IA:** Círculo dorado (#FFD700) en `cx="18", cy="8"` con radio 2.
