# MetodologIA Legal Compliance - Documentation

## 1. Strategic Purpose
Ensures legal transparency and trust. This directory contains the non-negotiable governance documents required for commercial operations and data handling.

## 2. Content Structure
- terminos.html: Terms of Service.
  - Usage rules for the website and services.
  - Intellectual property declarations.
- privacidad.html: Privacy Policy.
  - Data collection methods.
  - Rights of the users (Habeas Data).
  - GDPR alignment.

## 3. Keywords & Topics
- Privacy Policy
- Terms and Conditions
- Data Rights
- Legal Compliance

## 4. Maintenance
MUST be reviewed whenever a new lead collection tool is integrated or when the jurisdiction of operation expands. Sync with local and international data laws.
